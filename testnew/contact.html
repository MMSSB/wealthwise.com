Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is WealthWise completely free to use?

WealthWise offers both free and premium plans. The basic features are free for everyone, while advanced tools and personalized guidance are available with our premium subscription.

How secure is my financial data on WealthWise?

We take security very seriously. WealthWise uses bank-level encryption to protect your data, and we never sell your personal information to third parties. Our platform is regularly audited for security compliance.

Can I connect all my financial accounts to WealthWise?

Yes, WealthWise supports integration with thousands of financial institutions, including banks, credit cards, investment accounts, and loan providers. This allows you to see your complete financial picture in one place.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to your Account Settings and selecting "Manage Subscription." Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
